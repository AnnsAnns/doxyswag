USART Special Function Registers (SFR)

Despite being part of the USART peripheral, the SFR registers location is completely different. Even more confusing, the IE register of USART 1 follows the IE register of USART 0. Hence, the SFR register map of the two USART peripherals will overlap (with the IE register of the second USART being located at the first padding of the first USART). The padding bytes are intentionally declared as const to aid in preventing accidentally overwriting registers of the other USART's SFR registers.
